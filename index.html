Data Collection

The first step of the data science life cycle is data collection. After deciding on a topic, we gather information relevant to the topic from sources such as company databses or websites. In this tutorial we will be using a dataset from https://www.kaggle.com/ that contains data pertaining to Formula 1 World Championships that took place between 1950 - 2024. This dataset was created by a Principal Data Scientist on Kaggle named Vopani, who was inpired by the anount of data that has been collected and analyzed to design, build, and test drive Formula 1 cars. Vopani's Formaula 1 World Championship dataset can be found at https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohanrao/formula-1-world-championship-1950-2020
